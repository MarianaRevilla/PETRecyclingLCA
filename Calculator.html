How many kilograms of waste PET bottles were recycled?:

The following graphs show in blue the environmental impact that would have occured if the PET had not been recycled. The grey areas show the environmental benefit derived from the PET recycling, as you can see, recyling is the better option versus a traditional waste management scenario in Mexico.

Saved liters of water :

Recycling reduces the need to consume water, as no virgin resources are needed to produce new PET.

Saved kilograms of Carbon Dioxide:

All Greenhouse Gases are converted to Carbon Dioxide equivalents and then added up. For example, 1kg of nitrous oxide has the same effect as 298 kg of CO2. Thus, the unit for this impact category is CO2 eq.

Saved kilograms of Ozone-depleting substances:

Ozone Depleting substances are quantified relative to CFC-11, a powerful ozone-destructive substance. All ozone-destructive pollutants are converted to CFC-11 equivalents according to their chemical structure and lifetime.

Saved kilograms of toxic substances:

This impact category is measured in 1,4-DCB equivalents. Other toxic substances are compared to 1,4-DCB by dividing their potential impacts to those of 1,4-DCB and then added together.

Saved kilograms of eutrophying substances:

All eutrophying substances are compared to the potential effects of phosphorous on freshwater species. The unit for this impact category are kilograms of phosphorous equivalent.

Saved kilograms of acidifying substances:

This impact category is measured in equivalent kilograms of emitted SO2 to the air. The acidification potential values for different acidifying substances are calculated and then added together for a single score.
